Chikmagalur

Nature's Abode

Natural Beauty

Chikmagalur, nestled in the foothills of the Western Ghats, is renowned for its lush greenery, serene landscapes, and coffee plantations. It is a haven for nature lovers and adventure enthusiasts.

Attractions

Explore the breathtaking views from Mullayanagiri, the highest peak in Karnataka, or trek through the dense forests of Baba Budangiri. Visit the picturesque coffee estates and enjoy a cup of freshly brewed coffee.

Cultural Heritage

Experience the rich cultural heritage of Chikmagalur by visiting ancient temples like Sringeri Sharada Peetha and Horanadu Annapoorneshwari Temple. Dive into the history of the region at historical sites like Belavadi and Halebidu.

Adventure Activities

Engage in thrilling activities like trekking, camping, and wildlife safaris in the Bhadra Wildlife Sanctuary. Go on a scenic drive to Kemmangundi and witness the mesmerizing Hebbe Falls.
