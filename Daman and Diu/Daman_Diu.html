About Dadra and Nagar Haveli and Daman and Diu

The Union Territory of Dadra and Nagar Haveli and Daman and Diu is a unique blend of natural beauty, rich cultural heritage, and colonial architecture. Located on the western coast of India, this region offers lush greenery, tranquil beaches, and a fascinating mix of tribal and Portuguese heritage. Visitors can explore dense forests, wildlife sanctuaries, serene lakes, and historical forts, making it a perfect destination for nature lovers and history enthusiasts.
