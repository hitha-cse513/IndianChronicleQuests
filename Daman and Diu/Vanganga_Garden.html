About Vanganga Garden

Vanganga Garden, located in the charming town of Silvassa near Daman, is a picturesque garden that offers a peaceful retreat for visitors. The garden is known for its beautiful landscaping, vibrant flower beds, and serene water bodies. It's a perfect spot for leisurely walks, picnics, and relaxation amidst nature. The lush greenery and tranquil ambiance make Vanganga Garden a popular destination for both locals and tourists.

The garden features a variety of attractions, including walking paths, wooden bridges, and gazebos. Visitors can enjoy boating on the lake, explore the well-maintained lawns, and appreciate the diverse flora. Vanganga Garden is also a favorite spot for photography, offering numerous picturesque views and a refreshing escape from the hustle and bustle of city life.

Local Attractions

Vanganga Garden is surrounded by several attractions that showcase the natural beauty and cultural heritage of the region.

Silvassa Tribal Museum

The Silvassa Tribal Museum is a must-visit destination near Vanganga Garden. The museum exhibits the rich tribal culture and heritage of the local tribes, offering insights into their traditional lifestyle and crafts.

Dudhni Lake

Dudhni Lake, located a short drive from Vanganga Garden, is a stunning water body surrounded by hills and greenery. It offers boating, water sports, and a serene environment for relaxation and picnics.

Our Lady of Piety Church

Our Lady of Piety Church is a historical church located near Vanganga Garden. Built during the Portuguese era, the church features beautiful architecture and a peaceful atmosphere, making it a popular spot for visitors.

Hirwa Van Garden

Hirwa Van Garden is another beautiful garden in Silvassa, known for its lush greenery, fountains, and scenic landscapes. It's a perfect place for a leisurely stroll and enjoying nature.

Location

Vanganga Garden is situated in Silvassa, the capital of the Union Territory of Dadra and Nagar Haveli and Daman and Diu. The garden is easily accessible from Daman and other nearby cities, making it a convenient destination for visitors.

The best time to visit Vanganga Garden is during the cooler months from October to March, when the weather is pleasant and ideal for outdoor activities and exploration.

Reaching Vanganga Garden

Air

The nearest airport to Vanganga Garden is Chhatrapati Shivaji International Airport in Mumbai. From Mumbai, you can drive to Silvassa or take a domestic flight to the nearest airport in Surat.

Railways

Vapi Railway Station is the closest major railway station to Vanganga Garden, located about 18 kilometers away. Regular train services connect Vapi to various cities, and from Vapi, you can reach Silvassa by taxi or bus.

Road

Vanganga Garden is easily accessible by road from nearby cities. You can drive from Mumbai, Surat, or Ahmedabad, or take a bus to Silvassa and then use local transportation to reach the garden.
